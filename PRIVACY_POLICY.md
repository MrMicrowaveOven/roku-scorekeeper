# Privacy Policy

**Scorekeeper** — Roku Channel
Last updated: June 30, 2026

## Overview

Scorekeeper is a simple scorekeeping app for card games and tabletop games.
It runs entirely on your Roku device. It does not connect to the internet,
does not collect any personal information, and does not transmit any data
anywhere.

## Information We Collect

**None.**

Scorekeeper does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
information. It does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting
services, or any third-party libraries that collect data.

The only data the app handles are the player names and scores you enter
during a session. This data exists only in the app's memory while it is
running and is not written to any external service or persistent storage.

## Children's Privacy

Because Scorekeeper collects no data from any user, it is safe for users
of all ages, including children under 13. It fully complies with the
Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

## Third Parties

Scorekeeper does not share any information with third parties because it
does not collect any information in the first place.

## Changes to This Policy

If a future version of the app introduces data collection of any kind, this
policy will be updated before that version is released.
